Carrotour

A grid-based puzzle game where you utilize various "leaper" movements to navigate around obstacles and grab delicious carrots. Be a horse, camel, elephant or rabbit! Also, be prepared for intense boss battles featuring bullet hells!

Unfamiliar Leaper Movements

In this game, you don't just move up, down, left and right! Instead, you move as "leapers". They move in a specific angle about an axis, a fixed distance each time, and cannot be blocked. Think about the knight in chess, which is a classic leaper! Getting use to the move patterns is the key to victory.

Characters

Leappa

A curious and naughty rabbit, and a student of a local wizard school. Despite being a wizard, Leappa is really bad at magic, so bad that he failed the most basic magic class 3 times. However, Leappa is good at eating carrots, leaping around and solving puzzles... maybe.
